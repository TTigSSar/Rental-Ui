This Privacy Policy explains what personal data ToyShare collects, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data. We are committed to keeping your information safe and being transparent about our practices.

ToyShare is currently an MVP platform. Our data practices are minimal and focused only on what is needed to operate the service. This policy will be updated as the platform grows.

1. Data We Collect

Account Information

When you register, we collect your first name, last name, and email address. A hashed password is stored — we never store your plain-text password.

Listing Data

When you create a toy listing, we store the content you provide: toy name, description, photos, price, age range, condition, hygiene notes, and safety notes.

Usage Data

We may collect basic technical information such as your browser type, IP address, and the pages you visit, for the purpose of debugging and platform improvement. This data is not used for advertising.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Display your toy listings to other users.
  • Process and notify you of booking requests.
  • Send moderation decisions (listing approved / rejected) to your email.
  • Respond to support inquiries.
  • Maintain the security and integrity of the Platform.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Email Communications

We send transactional emails related to your account activity, including:

  • Listing approval or rejection notifications.
  • Booking request confirmations and updates.
  • Account-related security notifications.

We do not currently send marketing or promotional emails. If we do in the future, you will be given the option to opt out.

4. Authentication & Local Storage

We use JSON Web Tokens (JWTs) to authenticate your session. Your access token is stored in your browser's local storage to keep you signed in across page refreshes.

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies. Basic session management may use browser storage to improve usability.

5. Moderation & Logging

Our moderation team reviews all submitted toy listings before they are approved. Moderators see the full content of your listing, including photos and descriptions, for the purpose of ensuring platform quality and safety.

Platform activity may be logged for debugging, security monitoring, and improving the service. Logs are not shared externally and are retained only as long as necessary.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account and listing data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal information within a reasonable time, except where retention is required by law.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Object to processing of your data in certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the platform evolves. We will post the updated policy here and, where significant changes are made, notify you by email.

9. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us at privacy@toyshare.local.
