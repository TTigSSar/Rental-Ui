ToyShare started with a simple observation: children outgrow their toys quickly, yet new toys are expensive and often end up forgotten in storage. We built this platform to help families share the toys they already have — reducing waste, saving money, and giving every child access to a wider world of play.

Our goal is to make toy sharing as easy and trustworthy as borrowing from a neighbor you already know. Every listing is reviewed by our team, and every owner shares the condition, hygiene notes, and safety details of their toy before it goes live.

{{ 'info.about.moderationHeading' | translate }}

When a family lists a toy, it enters a pending review state and is not publicly visible until approved. Our moderation team checks each submission for:

  • Clear, accurate photos and descriptions
  • Age-appropriate and safe toys
  • Honest condition and hygiene information
  • Absence of prohibited or unsafe items

Once approved, the owner receives an email confirmation and the listing goes live. Listings that do not meet our standards are rejected with a clear reason, and owners can resubmit after making corrections.
